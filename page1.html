About The Line

The Line is a revolutionary urban development located in NEOM, Saudi Arabia. It is a linear city designed to accommodate millions of residents while ensuring a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Innovative Urban Living

Ideal Climate Year-Round

The Line has been meticulously designed to enjoy an ideal environment with sufficient spaces to create a local climate that maximizes sunlight, natural airflow, and shade while establishing a natural balance in air movement. Additionally, the open green spaces throughout the city will enhance comfort for all residents, visitors, and workers.

More Time with Loved Ones

Everyone will be able to access all city facilities within a 5-minute walk, and the public transport network within the city will allow journeys between the farthest points in the city in no more than 20 minutes. AI technologies will enhance all automated services and ensure convenient amenities within the city for residents, making it easy to meet with family, friends, and loved ones, and enjoy longer times together.

Fresh Air for Everyone

The city will be free from carbon emissions thanks to its abandonment of traditional infrastructure that heavily consumes carbon, such as cars and roads. The city will be powered entirely by 100% renewable energy, including powering its industries and sectors. The integration of nature with open spaces throughout the city will play a crucial role in air quality and purity.

Direct Access to Nature

Our advanced design will allow for quick and direct access to nature within two minutes of walking through its open spaces at multiple levels and elevations, where everyone can easily access the surrounding nature and enjoy its stunning landscapes, towering mountains, and clear skies, in a city liberated from urban sprawl due to reduced infrastructure impacts.

Zero Cars

Designed to reduce traffic congestion and air pollution.

100% Renewable Energy

The Line will run entirely on clean energy.

Smart City

Powered by artificial intelligence to ensure a futuristic lifestyle.
