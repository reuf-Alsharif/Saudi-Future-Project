Objectives
Establish new standards for public health and environmental protection.
Build a new model for sustainability.
Make NEOM a global destination for living and working, fostering a culture of exploration and adventure.
Develop and diversify the kingdom's economy, placing it on the global leadership map for development and innovation.

Additional Information

NEOM aims to revolutionize urban living by integrating advanced technologies and sustainable practices to create a better future.

The project will include a variety of sectors such as mobility, biotech, food, and entertainment, attracting global investment and talent.

More Information

NEOM is not just a project; it's a vision for the future. By integrating cutting-edge technologies and sustainable practices, NEOM will lead the way in urban living. The diverse sectors within NEOM, including biotech, food, and entertainment, will create a vibrant ecosystem attracting talent and investment worldwide.
