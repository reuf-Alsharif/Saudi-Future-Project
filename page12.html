ALHIJER

Admire the spirit of creativity that characterized the Nabataean era with the decorations carved with great skill on the tombs that embodied the gods, and discover the masterpieces of architecture from wells and water channels carved into the heart of the rocks.

Gramil

The Gramil area is distinguished by its pitch black night with millions of stars twinkling in the enchanting sky, and its brilliance reflected on the most wonderful rocky wonders.

HALL OF MIRRORS

It hosts many distinguished artistic and entertainment events to carve AlUla's name as a world-class cultural center. This architectural masterpiece is considered the largest mirror-covered building in the world.

ELEPHANT MOUNTAIN

Elephant Mountain perches on the golden sands of the desert with a breathtaking view, embracing the sky of AlUla with its body and trunk.
