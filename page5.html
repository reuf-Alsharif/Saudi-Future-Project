Welcome to Amaala

Amaala is a luxurious tourism destination located along the Red Sea coast of Saudi Arabia. Designed to reflect the beauty of the region, Amaala offers a unique blend of wellness, sustainability, and environmental preservation, making it a perfect destination for exclusive, eco-friendly experiences.

What is Amaala?

Amaala is an exciting luxury tourism development project located on the northwestern coast of Saudi Arabia. It is part of Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030, aimed at transforming the economy by boosting tourism and entertainment sectors. The project is centered on wellness, sustainability, and environmental conservation, catering to high-end travelers looking for an exclusive, eco-friendly experience.

Location

Amaala is located in the Prince Mohammed bin Salman Nature Reserve, along the Red Sea coast. The region is known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and stunning coral reefs. The natural beauty of this area makes it a perfect destination for luxury resorts and nature enthusiasts.

Amaala’s Vision

Amaala is designed to become a global hub for health, wellness, and sustainable luxury. The project focuses on integrating wellness tourism with medical treatments, holistic therapies, and personalized health services. Alongside these, it will offer cultural, artistic, and marine-related experiences, making it a multifaceted luxury destination.

For more information, visit the Saudi Vision 2030 website.
