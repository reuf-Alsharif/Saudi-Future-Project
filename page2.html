The Line City

A revolutionary urban concept in Neom

The Line is an innovative urban development project located in Neom, Saudi Arabia. It aims to create a sustainable city that redefines urban living.

Sustainable Design Smart Technologies Circular Economy More Information

Sustainable Design

The design of The Line focuses on sustainability and minimizing environmental impact.

Smart Technologies

The city incorporates smart technologies for efficient energy management and enhanced quality of life.

Circular Economy

The city promotes a circular economy to minimize waste and encourage resource reuse.
